About This Portfolio

This site showcases sample training materials, workflows, and SOP excerpts demonstrating how AI tools can enhance programme design and resource mobilization for NGOs working in humanitarian and development settings, including WASH, climate resilience, food security, and MCH/Nutrition in the Horn of Africa.

Consulting Focus

  • AI-assisted programme and proposal design for competitive donor calls.
  • AI-supported donor landscape analysis and opportunity scanning.
  • AI-enhanced digital fundraising campaigns and content optimization.
  • Responsible, ethical, and safe AI use aligned with global standards.
  • Development of practical templates, workflows, and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs).
