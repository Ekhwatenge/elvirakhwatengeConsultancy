Objective

Support communications and fundraising staff to use AI for audience insights, message generation, content testing, and basic analytics for digital fundraising, while keeping human oversight and ethical safeguards in place.

Sample Campaign Scenario

A digital fundraising campaign to support drought-affected households with climate-resilient WASH and livelihood interventions in the Horn of Africa.

  • Define target audiences (e.g., diaspora supporters, local private sector partners, global small donors).
  • Use AI to generate message variations tailored to each audience.
  • Develop email, social media, and landing page copy for A/B testing.
  • Ask AI to suggest story angles that center dignity, agency, and impact.

Example AI Use in the Workflow

  • Persona building: “Create 3 donor personas based on this supporter data and describe their motivations.”
  • Content drafting: “Draft three short social media posts for a fundraising appeal focused on climate resilience and clean water access.”
  • Optimization: “Suggest subject line A/B tests that are empathetic, specific, and non-extractive.”
  • Reporting: “Summarize campaign performance data into a narrative update for internal decision-makers.”

Deliverables (Illustrative)

  • Sample AI-assisted campaign brief (objectives, audiences, channels, KPIs).
  • Library of prompts for creative content, testing, and light analytics.
  • Checklist for safeguarding beneficiary dignity and data in digital campaigns.
